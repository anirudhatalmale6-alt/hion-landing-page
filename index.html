The Problem

Healthcare data is hopelessly fragmented

A patient sees 20 doctors in 10 years — but no single doctor ever sees the full history.

No Patient History

Records scattered across siloed clinics mean clinicians start every visit blind.

Missing Reports

Lab results and imaging vanish between providers, lost to paper and fax.

Duplicate Testing

Patients redo the same expensive tests because prior results can't be found.

Drug Interactions

Without a unified med list, conflicting prescriptions slip through unnoticed.

Insurance Fraud

No verifiable record trail lets duplicate and fabricated claims through.

Documentation Burden

Physicians spend up to two hours on paperwork for every hour of care.
